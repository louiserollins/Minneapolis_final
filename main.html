MINNEAPOLIS, MINNESOTA

City Snapshot

A major metropolis of the Midwest, Minneapolis is home to thriving urban cultural destinations such as award-winning restaurants, a lively arts scene, and plenty of nightlife. With over 100 local breweries, relaxation is always in reach after a day of exploring the many parks, rivers, and lakes that make Minneapolis famous.

Arts & Entertainment

Minneapolis is 2nd only to New York in live theaters per capita. Check out the current shows at The Guthrie for traditional theater, The Hennepin Theater Trust for contemporary options, or Jungle Theater for alternative options.

Lovers of art will find no shortage of diversions in Minneapolis. Among the many fine art exhibits scattered throughout the city, two museums to not miss are the Walker Art Center for modern art and MIA for traditional offerings.

Not to be left out, Minneapolis music also has a strong foothold. The famous First Ave venue with its star-studded wall is nestled in the heart of Downtown Minneapolis. Step into nearly any bar on a weekend night to discover up-and-coming local sounds, or tune in to local radio station 89.3FM The Current for a quick listen.

Outdoors

With a short 3-month summer, Minnesotans love to capitalize on the outdoors when they can! Summer days find people walking, biking, and roller-blading on well-manicured paths. Minneapolis has been counted among the most bike-friendly cities in the U.S. Several lakes can be found right in the heart of the city, perfect for sand volleyball, picnics, kayaking, or a leusiurely walk.

What To Expect

  • Weather in extremes - see below for packing advice for this moody climate
  • Plenty to do - boredom will not be a factor during a visit to Minneapolis

✔Packing List

Minnesota is a place of weather extremes. On a broad scale, the four seasons are well defined with a wet spring, hot, muggy summer, crisp fall, and bitter winter. Within each season, the weather can change by the day and even hour. What starts as a cloudy, crisp 60° day may transition to steamy, sunny, and 80° by noon; it's important to come prepared!
